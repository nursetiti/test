Effect of poverty

Issues like hunger, illness and thirst are both causes and effects of poverty.
Not having access to water means that you are poor, and being poor also
means that you may not be able to afford water or food either.
In other words, poor health, lack of water or housing, child abuse
or violence fuel a cycle of poverty in which so many end up trapped for life.
